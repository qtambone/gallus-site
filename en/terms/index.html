5. Important warning about alarm reliability

Gallus is designed to work robustly, but no alarm app can guarantee 100% delivery. Many factors outside our control can prevent an alarm from ringing correctly, including:

  • Phone turned off, dead battery, or hardware failure.
  • Airplane mode or Do Not Disturb configured incompatibly.
  • Phone volume at zero, headphones/Bluetooth blocking the speaker.
  • System permissions revoked (notifications, microphone, AlarmKit).
  • iOS/Android updates, hardware faults, force majeure.

Do not use Gallus as your only alarm for critical situations (flights, exams, medical appointments, on-call shifts, etc.). For any critical situation, set an independent backup alarm.
